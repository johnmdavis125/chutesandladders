Welcome to Chutes & Ladders!

How to Play:

There are 100 squares on the board and 2-4 players. The number of spaces a player advances each turn is determined by rolling a die once per turn. If the player lands at the bottom of a ladder, they immediately climb to the top. If the player lands at the top of a chute, they immediately slide to the bottom. The win state is reached when a player lands exactly on square 100, at which point the other players lose. The game will continue until this happens.
